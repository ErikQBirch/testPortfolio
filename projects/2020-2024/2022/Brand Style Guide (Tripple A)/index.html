Style Guide

Manifesto

Tripple A Web Studio strives to promote artists, animators, and authors who want to bring light to the world.

Voice & Tone

We use a tone and voice that is sincere and encouraging, yet also autoratitive and knowledgable. The feeling intended to portray is "You're an amazing creator who's got a lot of good work! We know how to help others see you this way too!"

Persona

Primary Audience

The primary audience consists of artist, animators, and authors who are new to the industry and want to have professional website portfolios to show off their work.

Pain Points

They don't want/can't afford a professional web designer and developer to build a site for them.
Their art skills are geared more towards their trade rather than web design.

Content Themes

Preview of the Week

The first post will be in the form of a still image that explains the what the video to follow will be about. I'll ask some questions concerning the topic for the week to encourage audience participation.

Highlight Reel

This will be the main focus post for the week. It will be a short video (3 Min. at max) that explains the importance of the Design, UI/UX, Content principle designated for the week.

Promotional Thanks

This will be another still image post with captions provided. The intent is to show appreciation towards any and those who participated for that week and to highlight their work. If none participate, just target one of the people I'm connected with on LinkedIn and hightlight their portfolio and how it works with the theme of the week.
